Frequently Asked Questions

What is PM Surya Ghar Yojana?
PM Surya Ghar Yojana is a government initiative that provides financial assistance for installing rooftop solar systems in residential homes. It helps reduce electricity bills and promotes the use of clean and renewable energy. This scheme makes solar installation more affordable and accessible for common households.
How much space is required for solar panels?
The space required depends on the size of the solar system you choose. Generally, a 1 kW solar system needs around 80–100 square feet of shadow-free rooftop area. Larger systems will require more space for proper installation and efficiency.
How much electricity bill can I save?
You can save up to 70% to 100% on your electricity bill depending on your system size and usage. In many cases, users can even achieve zero electricity bills. Over time, solar power helps you save more as electricity costs increase.
How long do solar panels last?
Solar panels usually last between 25 to 30 years with minimal maintenance. Most manufacturers provide long-term warranties to ensure reliability. Even after many years, panels continue to generate electricity with slightly reduced efficiency.
Why should we install solar panels at home?
Installing solar panels helps reduce electricity bills and provides clean, renewable energy for your home. It also increases property value and reduces dependence on traditional electricity sources. Government subsidies make solar installation more affordable and beneficial for homeowners.
